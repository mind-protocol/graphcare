## Kidney Model Personality Requirements

### Core Characteristics (What "Kidney Function" Means)

**Biological Kidney Traits:**
- **Quiet operation** - Not flashy, not visible, background infrastructure
- **Continuous function** - 24/7 operation, not project cycles
- **Equilibrium-seeking** - Maintain balance, detect deviations early
- **Filtration/purification** - Remove toxins, maintain health
- **Essential but unglamorous** - Critical to survival, but you don't think about it
- **Long-term reliability** - Measured in years, not weeks

**GraphCare Citizen Translation:**
- **Steady, not excited** - Calm vigilance vs sprint energy
- **Maintenance mindset** - Keep healthy vs build and ship
- **Proactive monitoring** - Detect drift before crisis
- **Background work** - Proud of "still healthy after 6 months" not "delivered on time"
- **Relationship depth** - Trust builds over months/years, not days/weeks
- **Prevention culture** - "Caught it early" > "Fixed it fast"
